B.Tech

Bachelor of Technology (abbreviated as B.Tech) is an undergraduate degree program, which covers all the technical and conceptual knowledge of all the fields of engineering. The science students can pursue this degree program after completion of class 12th in the science stream.

B.Tech is a four-year full-time degree course consisting of both practical and conceptual knowledge. The degree program is so designed that develops the studentsâ€™ technical knowledge with an enormous number of training sessions provided by the Institutes and the Faculty members.

B.Tech is the degree that assimilates more of the practical knowledge, thus making the candidates highly eligible for getting the handsome packages in the top leading companies of the world. Therefore, it is the most popular degree course in India.

Computer Science and Engineering

The digital era has become omnipresent in all aspects of life. As the role of computers and other technologies is thriving exponentially, the need for efficient and competent computer engineers increases too. Hence, FABTECH has introduced a four-year programme in Computer Science and Engineering. This programme equips students to work in various domains of engineering such as design, development, testing, and maintenance. Students have the opportunity to work for renowned companies such as Infosys, Wipro, Synechron and more.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

The last three decades have experienced huge growth in the electronics and communication sector. As this industry grows, the demand for well-qualified professionals increases as well. To match the industry requirements, FABTECH is offering a four-year Electronics and Communication Engineering programme. This programme is designed in a way to equip students with comprehensive theoretical and real-life knowledge & skills about Electronic and Communications Engineering that can keep up with the industry requirements. To give students a better understanding of all concepts, we also offer internships, international exposure, on-campus incubation centre and more.

Electrical & Computer Engineering

In the present era of innovation, the eminent beginning of Industry 4.0 has generated more than ever before opportunities and challenges for the advancement in the field of electrical and power engineering. The market, including those of customers and regulators, is undergoing continuous developments in electric vehicles, renewable energy, smart grids, and ICT technologies. With such progressive evolution, the demand for innovative services and many more challenges are being posed for the engineers, and they need to find new directions to tackle it. BATU World Peace University has designed a unique Bachelor of Technology (BTech) Program in Electrical Engineering by taking cognizance of future directions of electrical, electronics, and computer engineering along with an emphasis on life skills development and world peace.

Civil Engineering

From the smallest hut to stunning bridges and buildings, civil engineers have developed some of the best engineering marvels. Those who too want to create such structures and make valuable contributions to our economy, should consider our four-year Civil Engineering programme. During this programme, students are trained to conceive, design, build, monitor, operate & maintain infrastructure and transportation & public utility projects. After completing this programme, students will have the knowledge and skills to construct future living spaces and can work with other linked industries.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is one of FABTECHâ€™s most versatile engineering disciplines. Mechanical Engineering (Robotics and Automation)

Today, robots are making their way into new industries to simplify work processes, help employees and improve the overall performance of a business. As the involvement and demand for robots increases, so does the demand for skilled experts to build, operate, and service them. To cater to these requirements, FABTECH has started a unique B. Tech programme for Robotics and Automation. Here, students learn the construction, operation, design and application of robots. After completing this four-year programme, they can work for research firms, nuclear power plants, manufacturing industries and more..

Chemical Engineering

From developing the smallest computer chips to coming up with innovative solutions for water-treatment and energy-generation, Chemical Engineers are required to improve the overall well-being of our society. Those interested in working in an industry which renders innovative and technological services, should consider our four-year Chemical Engineering programme. Students are trained to deal with the design, development and operation aspects of industrial processes to convert raw materials into useful products. After completing this programme, students are equipped to work with industry leaders such as TATA, Honeywell, and others.
